A Bit About Me

I'm a Product Manager with a Computer Science background specializing in AI and data science. What drives me is the challenge of taking complex technical capabilities and making them accessible to people who need them most—whether that's enabling business teams to build AI agents without code or helping developers integrate faster.

I believe the best products come from talking to users weekly, not monthly. I've learned that saying "no" gracefully is more valuable than saying "yes" to every feature request. And I'm passionate about measuring what matters—not vanity metrics, but real user outcomes.
