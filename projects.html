Animake – Anime Creator Marketplace

Side project · Product, Design, Architecture

Concept and early design for an anime-focused creator marketplace under a small studio brand. Focused on building trust for buyers and sellers, clear multi-tenant flows, and a scalable data model.

My role

  • Defined the problem, target users, and use cases.
  • Created user journeys for buyers and sellers.
  • Designed the core database schema (orders, payouts, variants, etc.).

Impact & learnings

  • Clarified what a “minimum lovable” marketplace experience could look like.
  • Explored operational costs, fees, and long-term sustainability.

Machine Translation Analytics & TM Tooling

Work / Side project · Internal analytics & UX

Concept for a machine translation platform analytics dashboard with glossary and translation memory management, built to help teams understand usage and improve content quality.

My role

  • Defined metrics and events for tracking user behaviour.
  • Mapped GA dashboard layouts and visualizations.
  • Outlined requirements for glossary and TM management flows.

Impact & learnings

  • Provided a clear DRD structure for future implementation.
  • Connected product decisions back to measurable behaviour and quality.
